Benefits of the Calculator

Estimate Energy Production

The Calculator allows you to estimate how much energy your solarpanels are likely to produce on a monthly basis. This helps with sizing the syste, and knowing your return on investment

Reduce Your Carbon Footprint

Every kilowatt-hour of electricity from your solar panels means one less from dirty or polluting fuels. The calculators information can help you do your part to address climate change and help create a cleaner environment for future generations.

Reduce Electricity Prices

The information given from the calculator allows you to lock in your electricity rate today with affordable solar and insulate yourself from future price hikes from other sources of power.
